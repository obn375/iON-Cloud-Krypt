## iON by Krypt Introduction

iON by Krypt is the cloud VPS division of Krypt Technologies, a hosting provider that's been doing this since 1998. They're part of the Evocative family now, which means they've got some serious infrastructure backing them up.

What makes iON interesting is their focus on flexibility. You get full control over your cloud servers with features like instant reboots, HTML5 console access, and the ability to mount ISOs or switch between operating systems without waiting for support tickets. Everything's automated, which is refreshing if you've ever dealt with hosting providers that make you wait hours for simple changes.

Their network setup is pretty solid too. They use KVM (Kernel-based Virtual Machine) technology, which is open-source and gives you more customization options than some alternatives. Plus, they offer multiple network route options including CN2 for China connectivity—handy if you're targeting Asian markets or need low-latency connections to mainland China.

The company operates data centers in strategic locations across the US (Los Angeles, San Jose, Dallas, Honolulu) and Singapore. Each location has its own pricing and bandwidth configurations, so you'll want to pick based on where your users actually are rather than just going for the cheapest option.

One thing to note: iON by Krypt positions itself as a more technical, hands-on solution. They're not holding your hand through WordPress installations or offering one-click app setups like some consumer-focused hosts. You get root access and you're expected to know what to do with it. If that sounds intimidating, this might not be your first choice. But if you want control without artificial limitations, that's exactly the point.

Support seems to be email-based with a ticketing system. Don't expect 24/7 live chat or phone support that you get with premium managed hosting.
